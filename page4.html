Cigarettes After Sex


Acoording to Allmusic, Cigarettes After Sex or CAS, is an "Ambient pop collective" that came to be when the songwriter of the band, Greg Gonzalez, experimented on a sound project.

The band of four has been around for 15 years, and still counting, accidentally created in 2008 at El Paso, Texas. This happened when a student of the University of Texas was experimenting in the four story hallway of the university, trying to record space like audio.

The band has been applauded and loved for its unique ethreal feel. The uniqueness of the ambient music and its relatable lyrics is one of the many factors to the bands rise to success. The band is now the staple of genre like dreamcore, indierock, slowcore and shoegaze.

From the accidental discovery of the band to now a well known global sensation. Their biggest song being "Apocalypse" with more that one million streams.

Their latest songs being a single called "Tejano Blue" and an "Bubblegum". The band now has 24,940,286 monthly listeners in counting.
